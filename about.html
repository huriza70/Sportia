Who we are:

Stadia5 is a dynamic and forward thinking firm with foundations that run deeper and stronger in the sport industry. Established in 2005, Stadia5 has provided its expertise in many international events namely MatchWorld Cup, Dubai International Bowling Tournament, Dubai International Sports Conference etc. Along with this Stadia5 has also provided its services for FINA, Soccerex, FIFA Beach Soccer and many more.
