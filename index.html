About Stadia5 SSC

Stadia5 is a dynamic and forward thinking firm with foundations that run deeper and stronger in the sport industry. Established in year 2005, Stadia5 has gained expertise in organising and managing many different kinds of Sport Events.

Consultancy Services: Stadia5 also offers specialised sports consulting services. We aim to provide effective, innovative and strategic player management and consulting services and solutions for the global sports industry. With both its local based firm and global perspective, Stadia5 seeks to facilitate the development and progression of its sports athletes and clients worldwide.

Events: Stadia5 specializes in organizing and managing events, and provides Complete Solutions for any kinds of Sporting Events and Social and Corporate Events, Product Launches, Gala Dinners, Exhibitions, Conferences and various event related services.

Summer Camps: Stadia5 organises Summer Camps, Goal keeper training & workshops for local clubs in the Middle East & Europe. We offer specialised tailor made training camps for Sports Clubs and teams from different parts of the world.

Leagues &Tournaments: Stadia5 organises Football, Futsal Tournaments, Cricket & Basketball Tournaments on a regular basis. Contact us now to find out how you can be a part of it.
